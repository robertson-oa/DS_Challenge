Dataset Overview¶

This data set includes information about 60 Participants human behaviour recognition that recorded sensory data using devices such as smart phones (ipohone and andriods) and smart watches. Sensors included:acclerometer, gyroscope, magnetometer, watch acclerometer, locataion, audio etc.The labels in the dataset includes; physical activites(e.g Walking, running), Daily activities(eg: sleeping) Locations(eg: School, Work, Home). The dataset is in csv format which can be. Downloaded here.
Visualization are created from preliminary wrangling and cleaning process to facilitate exploration analysis to discover usage pattern.

INTRODUCTION¶

  • Human Activity Recognition (HAR): Recognize the activity being performed by an individual at a given moment
  • Human Context Recognition (HCR): Recognize the context (environment, location, interaction) thatthe activityis being performed.
  • The combination of the two is important for the understanding of the human activities
  • Wide variety of applications related to personal assistance and recommendations
